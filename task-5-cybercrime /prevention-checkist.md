# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

As college students, we use the internet and online payments almost every day, so it’s important to stay alert and protect ourselves from scams. Here are some simple and practical tips:

## General Safety Tips

* Never share your passwords, OTPs, or personal details with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank or company.
* Avoid clicking on unknown links sent through SMS, email, or social media—they can be fake or harmful.
* Always double-check who you are talking to before trusting any call or message.
* Use strong passwords and try not to keep the same password for all accounts.
* Turn on two-factor authentication (2FA) for extra security.
* Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into important accounts like banking or email.
* Keep your phone and apps updated to stay protected from security issues.

## Financial / UPI Safety Tips

* Never accept unknown “collect requests” on UPI apps without checking properly.
* Remember, to receive money you don’t need to enter your UPI PIN or approve any request.
* Always verify the name or UPI ID before sending money.
* Try to use only trusted apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm.

## Reporting Cybercrime

* If you face any fraud, report it immediately on the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
* You can also call the Cyber Crime Helpline at 1930 for quick help in financial fraud cases.

Staying a little careful and aware can help us avoid major problems and keep our money and data safe.
