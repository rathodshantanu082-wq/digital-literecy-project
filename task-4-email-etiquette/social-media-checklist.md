# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

## ✅ Do’s

* Think before you post anything online.
* Keep your profile private or limited to trusted people.
* Use strong passwords and enable security settings.
* Share useful, positive, and meaningful content.
* Respect others’ opinions and communicate politely.

## ❌ Don’ts

* Don’t share personal information like phone number, address, or bank details.
* Don’t click on unknown or suspicious links.
* Don’t post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
* Don’t accept friend requests from unknown people.
* Don’t spend too much time on social media and ignore studies.

---

Being responsible on social media helps us maintain a good image and stay safe online.
