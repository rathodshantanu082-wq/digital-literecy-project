## 📝 Task Details

### 📊 Task 1: Digital Literacy Infographic

In this task, I created an infographic using Canva to explain the concept of digital literacy.

The infographic includes:

* Meaning of digital literacy
* Useful tools for students (GitHub, LinkedIn, Google Docs)
* Safe internet practices
* Professional online presence
* Email etiquette

This task helped me learn how to present information visually in a simple and clear way.

---

### 💻 Task 2: Digital Profiles

In this task, I created and updated my profiles on:

* GitHub
* LinkedIn
* HackerRank

These platforms are important for building a strong professional presence. I learned how to showcase my skills, add information, and maintain a clean profile.

This will help me in internships and placements in the future.

---

### 🌐 Task 3: Coding & Collaboration Tools

This task involved:

* Solving a beginner problem on HackerRank
* Creating a Google Form (Digital Literacy Quiz)
* Viewing responses in Google Sheets

Through this task, I understood how online tools can be used for learning, collaboration, and data collection.

---

### 📧 Task 4: Professional Communication

In this task, I worked on communication skills by:

* Writing a formal email to a professor (extension request)
* Writing an email for internship application
* Creating a social media Do’s and Don’ts checklist

This helped me understand how to communicate professionally and maintain a good digital image.

---

### 🔐 Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness

In this task, I studied **UPI and online payment frauds**.

I created:

* A case study explaining how fraud happens
* A prevention checklist for students

Key learnings:

* Never share OTP
* Always verify payment requests
* Be aware of scams

This task improved my awareness about online safety and responsible digital behavior.
